Ingredients:

  • Paneer: 200g
  • Butter: 2 tbsp
  • Tomatoes: 4 (pureed)
  • Onion: 1 (finely chopped)
  • Cream: 3 tbsp
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Cashew paste: 2 tbsp
  • Spices: Turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, kasuri methi, salt
  • Oil: 1 tbsp

Steps for preperation:

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan, saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add cashew paste, spices, and mix well.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Finish with cream and kasuri methi. Serve hot with naan or rice.
