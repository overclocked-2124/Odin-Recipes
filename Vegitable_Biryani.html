Ingredients:

  • Basmati rice: 2 cups
  • Mixed vegetables: 2 cups (carrot, peas, beans, etc.)
  • Onion: 2 (sliced)
  • Yogurt: 1/2 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Spices: Bay leaf, cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala
  • Saffron: A pinch (soaked in milk)
  • Oil: 2 tbsp
  • Ghee: 1 tbsp

Steps for preparation:

  1. Cook rice until 70% done. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, saut√© spices, and onions until golden.
  3. Add vegetables, yogurt, and spices. Cook until veggies soften.
  4. Layer rice and vegetable mix in a pot. Drizzle saffron milk and ghee.
  5. Cover and cook on low heat for 20 minutes. Serve with raita.
