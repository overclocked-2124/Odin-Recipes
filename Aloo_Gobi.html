Ingredients:

  • Potatoes: 2 (cubed)
  • Cauliflower florets: 2 cups
  • Tomatoes: 2 (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Spices: Turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, garam masala, cumin seeds, salt
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

Steps for preparation:

  1. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, and saut√© ginger-garlic paste.
  2. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  3. Add spices, potatoes, and cauliflower. Mix well.
  4. Cover and cook on low heat until vegetables are tender.
  5. Garnish with coriander leaves. Serve with roti or rice.
