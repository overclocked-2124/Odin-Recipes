Ingredients:

  • Chickpeas: 1 cup (soaked overnight)
  • Tomatoes: 3 (pureed)
  • Onion: 2 (finely chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Spices: Chole masala, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin seeds, garam masala, salt
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

Steps for preparation:

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas until soft.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, onions, and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© until golden.
  3. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in spices and cooked chickpeas. Simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with bhatura or rice.
