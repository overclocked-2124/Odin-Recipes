Ingredients:

  • Spinach: 250g
  • Paneer: 200g
  • Onion: 1 (finely chopped)
  • Tomatoes: 2 (pureed)
  • Ginger-garlic paste: 1 tsp
  • Cream: 2 tbsp
  • Spices: Turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, salt
  • Oil: 2 tbsp

Steps for preparation:

  1. Blanch spinach and puree it.
  2. Heat oil, saut√© onions, and ginger-garlic paste.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Stir in spinach puree and spices. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add paneer cubes and cream. Cook for 2 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with roti or naan.
